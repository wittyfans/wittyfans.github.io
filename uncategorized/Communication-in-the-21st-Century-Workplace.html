Week 1

Why you Communication in 21st ?

  1. Give instructions / 指导
  2. Convey facts / 指出事实
  3. Give advice / 给予建议
  4. Give ideas and gather ideas / 分享和收集点子
  5. Persuade or influence others / 说服或者影响别人
  6. To negotiate / 谈判
  7. To manage and resolve conflict / 管理或者解决争论
  8. To gain an understanding and appreciation of other’s points of views / 增进对新点子的了解
  9. To motivate others / 动员别人
  10. To express your feelings and emotions / 传达你的感受和情感
  11. To control and co-ordinate efforts / 管理或合作事宜

Communicating With Peers Part 1

不同的工作有不同的交流方式，但只要是有效的沟通，都能给你的工作带来好处，不管是提高产品的销量，还是增加顾客满意度。 这门课中介绍了有5种不同的沟通方式，Verbal(口头的) 和 nonverbal, internal communication(组织内沟通) 和 External communication（组织外沟通）， in-person communication（个人）.

Communicating With Peers Part 2

Part2 介绍了一下与你的同事交流的技巧，但在这些技巧之下有一个非常重要，管用的元素，即connecte through communication.
首先要做的就是考虑你的听众，你对和你沟通的对象了解多少？他们很喜欢交谈吗？还是喜欢长话短说？其次考虑自己的面部表情，手势动作，营造一种开放的交流环境，比如张开手臂，把话题传递给别人，眼睛跟随说话的人。笑也是一种很有用的方式，尽量让自己积极自然的笑。Keep it simple, say it slowly, and have a smile.

tips

what do to with a venter/screamer

  • I can see you’re really frustrated. To make sure I don’t add to that, and to make sure I don’t miss something, what was the most important thing I need to do in the long term, what’s the critical thing I need to do in the short term, and what do I need to get done ASAP. What you just said is way too important for me to have misunderstood a word, so I’m going to say it back to you to make sure I am on the same page with you. Here’s what I heard.” Then repeat exactly, word for word, what they said to you. After you finish, say to them, “Did I get that right and if not, what did I miss?” Forcing them to listen to what you said they said, “because it was important,” will slow them down, will help you stay centered and in control, and will earn you their and your own respect.
